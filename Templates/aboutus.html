About Us

Connecting Students and Alumni for a Stronger Tomorrow

Why We Exist

ULAB Alumni Connect is built to bridge the gap between current students and alumni. Our goal is simple: to build meaningful relationships, share opportunities, and create a strong support network across generations of ULABians.

Whether you're a student seeking guidance or an alum looking to give back — this is your space to connect, inspire, and grow together.

Our Mindset

We believe that great things happen when people stay connected. By encouraging open dialogue, mentorship, and collaboration, we ask 'What if we stay in touch?' and answer with 'Why not make it impactful?'

Our Vision

To foster lifelong bonds between students and alumni that go beyond graduation. From networking events and mentorship programs to knowledge-sharing platforms — ULAB Alumni Connect is a movement toward a future of unity and shared success.
